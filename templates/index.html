Welcome to AutoNotes

This project uses a neural network that was built with Tensorflow, along with some OpenCV pre-processing to do its best to read your handwriting. Currently the neural network's accuracy isn't the greatest (turns out handwriting is one of the leading issues in ML) but it's definitely fun to play with! For best results, ensure your images are on a plain white background with even lighting.
